## Kigali's Live Music Scene

Kigali's restaurant scene comes alive after dark. While the city is known for its quiet, safe streets, several venues offer live music that transforms a dinner into an evening out.

## 1. Tania's Cuisine & Lounge — Gishushu

Live band every Friday and Saturday night, plus a resident DJ. The [Cigar Lounge](https://taniascuisine.rw/lounge) offers a more intimate setting for after-dinner drinks. The music leans toward Rwandan, East African, and Afrobeat — perfect with a plate of grilled tilapia.

**Best for:** Groups who want dinner + drinks + dancing under one roof.

## 2. Repub Lounge — Kimihurura

Rooftop venue with Afro-fusion music. Popular with Kigali's young professionals.

## 3. Crystal Lounge — Kacyiru

Live band on weekends, known for its outdoor terrace.

## 4. La Creola — Kimihurura

Rooftop tapas and live music. More of a lounge atmosphere.

## Plan Your Night

Start with dinner at [Tania's](https://taniascuisine.rw/contact) — try the daily buffet or order from the full menu — then settle into the lounge for live music. Reserve your table by WhatsApp.
