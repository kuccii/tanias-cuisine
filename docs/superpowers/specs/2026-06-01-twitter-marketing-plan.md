# Twitter Marketing Plan — Tania's Cuisine & Lounge (@LoungeTania)

## Overview
Daily Twitter content strategy for Tania's Cuisine & Lounge — a restaurant, lounge, and catering business in Kigali, Rwanda. Goals: brand awareness + driving reservations/orders.

Audience: Local Kigali residents, expats/diplomats, tourists planning trips to Rwanda.

Posting cadence: once daily at 11:30 AM Kigali time (pre-lunch window).

---

## Weekly Content Pillars

| Day | Theme | Content | Goal |
|-----|-------|---------|------|
| Mon | #MenuMonday | One dish photo + price + 2-line description | Drive food cravings |
| Tue | #VibeTuesday | Lounge/atmosphere shot — dim lighting, decor | Attract lounge visitors |
| Wed | #CateringWednesday | Event setup or buffet spread | Book event inquiries |
| Thu | #TasteOfKigali | Area photo or drink shot + local angle | Expat/tourist awareness |
| Fri | #WeekendReady | Live music, weekend special, or chef's pick | Drive weekend reservations |
| Sat | #SaturdayVibes | Casual lounge scene, guests enjoying | FOMO / social proof |
| Sun | #SundaySlowDown | Coffee/tea shot + relaxing caption | Coffee bar traffic |

---

## Visual Strategy
- Use area photos from `images/area/` as primary image bank
- Supplement with phone photos of actual dishes (authentic > polished)
- Consistent warm/golden edit tone across all images
- No stock photos

## Caption Formula (2-4 lines max)
```
[Dish/Scene name] at Tania's 🍽️
[One short line about flavor/atmosphere]
[CTA — book, visit, or tag]
📍 KG 8 Ave, Remera
```

### Example
> Beef Fillet Steak at Tania's 🥩
> Mushroom, peppercorn, or garlic sauce — prime cuts grilled over open flame.
> Reserve your table: taniascuisine.rw
> 📍 KG 8 Ave, Remera

## Engagement Tactics
- Reply to every comment within 2 hours
- Retweet Kigali food/city accounts to cross-pollinate
- Tag @VisitRwanda and @CityOfKigali on relevant posts
- Use 1-2 hashtags max: `#KigaliFood` `#KigaliRestaurants`

## Growth Mechanics
- Follow 50 relevant Kigali accounts/day
- One DM reply or quote-retweet/week of a Kigali food post
- Cross-promote Instagram (@taniacuisineandlounge) → Twitter on weekends

## Posting Logistics
- One post/day at 11:30 AM Kigali time (~10 min/day)
- Batch-create: 30 min every Sunday to select 7 images + write 7 captions
- Tools: phone camera only. Optional: Canva for story-style graphics

## 30-Day Ramp
| Week | Focus |
|------|-------|
| Week 1 | Post 7 days straight to re-activate dormant followers |
| Week 2-4 | Stick to daily theme, reply to comments, follow 50/day |
| End of month | Review top 3 posts by engagement, double down |

## Success Metrics
- Impressions/post (target: 500+ by week 4)
- Profile visits/week
- DMs/mentions requesting reservations
- Follower growth (target: +100/month)
