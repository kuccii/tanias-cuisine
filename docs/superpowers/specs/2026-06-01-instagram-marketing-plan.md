# Instagram Marketing Plan — Tania's Cuisine & Lounge (@taniacuisineandlounge)

## Overview
Instagram-first content strategy for Tania's Cuisine & Lounge — restaurant, lounge, and catering in Kigali, Rwanda. Instagram is the primary visual storefront: high discovery via Reels, location tags, and hashtags.

**Handle:** @taniacuisineandlounge
**Website:** taniascuisine.rw
**Locations:** KG 8 Ave, M&M Plaza, Remera — KG 9 Ave, Kimihurura
**Audience:** Local Kigali residents, expats/diplomats, tourists planning Rwanda trips

---

## Posting Cadence
- **Feed posts:** 3-4x/week (Mon, Wed, Fri, Sun)
- **Reels:** 2x/week (Thu, Sat — plus repurpose feed posts as Reels)
- **Stories:** Daily (behind-the-scenes, daily special, repost tagged content)
- **Best time:** 11:30 AM Kigali time (pre-lunch), with Stories also at 6:30 PM (pre-dinner)

---

## Visual Strategy

### Feed Aesthetic
- Warm golden/amber edit tone — consistent preset across all images
- Dark, moody backgrounds with warm lighting (match lounge atmosphere)
- No stock photos — only 2 sources:
  - Professional area photos from `images/area/`
  - Phone photos of actual dishes and lounge scenes
- Carousel posts: 5-7 slides, first slide is the hero/cover

### Reel Style
- 15-30 seconds max
- Slow-mo close-ups of food prep, sauce pours, grill flames
- Text overlay: dish name + price in bottom third
- Trending audio + restaurant audio (sizzle, clink, music)
- No voiceover needed — let visuals + text + ambient sound carry it

### Story Format
- Phone photo + 2-line text overlay
- Location tag + hashtag sticker
- Poll/emoji slider on food stories for engagement
- Repost customer tagged stories to Stories

## Growth Mechanics

### Organic
- Location tag **every** post (KG 8 Ave, Remera)
- Reply to all comments within 2 hours
- Engage with 20 Kigali food/business accounts/day (like + genuine comment)
- Tag @visitrwanda_now and @cityofkigali on relevant posts
- Cross-promote Twitter → Instagram in Stories on weekends

### Collabs & Influencer
- 1x/month: invite a Kigali food micro-influencer (2K-10K followers) for a complimentary meal in exchange for a Reel + Story
- 1x/quarter: collab with a hotel or tour operator for cross-promotion (they recommend Tania's to guests)

### Paid (optional — Phase 2)
- Boost top-performing Reels monthly (budget: $20-50/mo)
- Target: Kigali + expat interests (aged 24-55)
